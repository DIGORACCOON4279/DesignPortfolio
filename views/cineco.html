Context and Challenge

Context and Challenge

Context:

Project Type: Redesign of a ticket booking and purchasing website.

Main Objective: Improve user experience in the process of booking and purchasing movie tickets.

Client/User Description: Users seeking a quick and easy experience for booking and purchasing movie tickets online.

Challenge:

Design Challenges: Create an intuitive and attractive interface that works well on desktop devices.

Constraints: Keep the design within the brand's style guide and ensure high accessibility for all users.

Research and Discovery

Research and Discovery

Research Methods:

Benchmarking: Comparison with three leading movie ticket booking sites.

User Personas: Creation of detailed personas to represent different user types.

User Interviews: Interviews with frequent users of the current site to understand their frustrations and desires.

Usability Testing: Usability testing with initial prototypes to identify navigation and comprehension issues.

Key Findings:

Benchmarking: Identification of successful features and designs in competitor sites.

User Personas: Understanding the diverse needs and behaviors of users.

User Interviews: Identification of common issues, such as difficulty finding movies and slow load times.

Usability Testing: Improvement of navigation and simplification of the booking process.

Conceptualization and Design

Conceptualization and Design

Methodological Approach:

Use of Design Thinking to iterate quickly on design ideas.

Design Methodology:

An agile approach based on Design Thinking was applied to understand user needs, prototype rapid solutions, and iteratively validate the interface design.

User Flows:

Design of clear and efficient user flows using Whimsical.

Wireframes:

Creation of low and mid-fidelity wireframes in Figma.

Wireflows:

Creation of mid-fidelity wireflows in Figma.

Prototypes:

Development of interactive prototypes in Figma for user testing.

Implementation and Results

Implementation and Results

Development

Implementation of the new interface

User Flows:

Two main flows were developed; one for booking tickets without logging in and another for continuing a purchase started at another time, saving and reminding the user of their last visit.

Results:

Reduction of average booking time by 30%. Increase in user satisfaction according to post-launch surveys.

Feedback:

Positive feedback from users highlighting ease of use and modern aesthetics.

Impact and Benefits

Significant improvement in user experience and booking process efficiency.
