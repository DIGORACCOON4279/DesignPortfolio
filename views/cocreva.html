Context and Challenge

Context and Challenge

The MVP development for COCREVA faced initial challenges due to the lack of clear definition of the services the client wished to offer. As we gathered more client information, the project evolved and became better defined.

Research and Discovery

Research and Discovery

We conducted thorough research and hypothesis validations initially, but the initial lack of clarity led us to redesign towards a more basic yet user-focused landing page. The first user testing revealed the need for clearer, more direct communication aligned with user expectations.

Conceptualization and Design

Conceptualization and Design

Based on new users insights and findings from validations, I led the UX/UI design efforts. We iterated through wireframes and mockups that reflected the project's new direction. We focused on a simplified but informative structure for the landing page, strategically placing each element to capture user attention effectively.

Implementation and Results

Implementation and Results

We delivered the UX/UI design a week ahead of schedule to the development team. This allowed for final adjustments and ensured the landing page was ready for a timely and successful launch.

Achievements and Learnings

Achievements and Learnings

This project underscored the importance of swiftly adapting to changes in client needs and market dynamics. We learned valuable lessons in clear communication and well-defined page structure, which significantly enhanced user experience and product effectiveness.

Teamwork and Communication

Effective teamwork and clear communication across departments and individual contributors were crucial to navigating uncertainties and achieving project milestones. Each team and individual played a vital role in ensuring alignment with project objectives and client expectations.

Impact and Benefits

Impact and Benefits

The redesigned landing page received positive feedback from end users, who found it easier and clearer to understand the offered services. This demonstrated our ability to navigate shifting requirements and deliver project goals efficiently.
